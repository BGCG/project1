Love Edinburgh

There are endless activities to do in Edinburgh. This page could not even list them all as there is so much!

Edinburgh Festival Fringe

Edinburgh festival happens every august and hosts a wealth of theatre, comedy and circus performances. The city is full of creative energy every august where performers and tourist travels all over the world to perform or see the many shows. Want a place for food and drink during the festival? Go to George square gardens where there are a variety of food and drinks stalls to meet anyones desires!

Edinburgh international film festival

Edinburgh international film festival happens every august and hosts a wealth of avante garde films from independent film makers. It's a must see!
