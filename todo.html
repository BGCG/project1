Events in Edinburgh

Edinburgh Festival Fringe

Edinburgh Festival Fringe happens every August and hosts a wealth of theatre, comedy and circus performances. The city is full of creative energy every August where performers and tourists travels all over the world to perform or see over 4000 plus shows in the city.

Edinburgh International Film Festival

Edinburgh International Film Festival happens every August and hosts a wealth of avante garde films from independent film makers. It's a must see!

Edinburgh International Book Festival

Edinburgh International Book Festival happens every year where writers and literary lovers descend on the city and promote and share their literature. A must go for avid readers!

Edinburgh music festivals

Edinburgh hosts a number of music festivals to provide for all your music needs. If you enjoy pop and rock music head to the Connect festival. If you are a dance music lover why not check out Terminal V at the Royal Highland Centre.
