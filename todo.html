Love Edinburgh

There are endless activities to do in Edinburgh. The key ones are listed below...

Edinburgh Festival Fringe

Edinburgh festival happens every august and hosts a wealth of theatre, comedy and circus performances. The city is full of creative energy every august where performers and tourists travels all over the world to perform or see the many shows. Want a place for food and drink during the festival? Go to George square gardens where there are a variety of food and drinks stalls to meet anyones desires!

Edinburgh international film festival

Edinburgh international film festival happens every august and hosts a wealth of avante garde films from independent film makers. It's a must see!

Edinburgh international book festival

Edinburgh internation book festival happens every year where writers are invited to come and promote their literature. A must go for avid readers!

Edinburgh music festivals

Edinburgh hosts a number of music festivals to provide for all your music needs. If you enjoy pop and rock music head to the CONNECT festival. If you are a dance music lover why not check out Terminal V at the royal highland centre.
