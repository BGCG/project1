Why visit Edinburgh?

Edinburgh is an old historic city located in Southeastern Scotland with many historical landmarks and things to do for all age groups. It is the Capital of Scotland and is home to Edinburgh castle, a popular tourist destination for many people round the world. Love Edinburgh is here to provide recommendations of the best restaurant and events to visit in and around Edinburgh.
